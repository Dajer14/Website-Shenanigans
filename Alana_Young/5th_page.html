Pros and Cons



Pros
Cacti and succulents don't cost much.
They won't die of they aren't religiously watered.
They liven up drab rooms.
There are thousands to choose from.
They can be purchased nearly anywhere.
They are easy to care for.
Cons
Sometimes, you get stabbed trying to water them.
Eventually, you run out of room for the 20-some plants
that decorate your countertops.
It seems to take forever watering them.
If you have cats,
they always try to knock the plants onto the floor.
